About the Artist

Divine.
Unstoppable.

Born Divine Joshua Ikubor in Benin City, Nigeria, Rema emerged from humble beginnings to become one of the most electrifying voices in global music. Raised by his mother after losing his father and elder brother, he channeled grief into art — and the world listened.

At just 19, a viral Instagram freestyle caught the attention of D'Prince, landing him a deal with Jonzing World — a subsidiary of Don Jazzy's legendary Mavin Records. His debut EP immediately topped Apple Music Nigeria.

Rema invented Afro-Rave — a genre-defying fusion of Afrobeats, Arabian and Indian influences, house, and hip-hop. His sound doesn't follow trends. It creates them.
